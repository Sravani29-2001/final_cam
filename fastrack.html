Personalized Placement Prep

Become Self-Sufficient for Your Dream Job!

Our comprehensive placement training program is designed to equip you with the skills and confidence you need to excel in your job search. Through a series of weekly workshops, webinars, and mini-courses, we cover a range of essential topics to make you self-sufficient and independent in your placement preparation journey.

  • Resume Building - Learn how to create an effective resume that highlights your skills and achievements.
  • Interview Preparation - Get ready for your job interviews with mock interviews, feedback sessions, and tips on handling common interview questions.
  • Improving Body Language and Communication Skills - Enhance your non-verbal communication and interpersonal skills to make a positive impression during interviews.
  • How to Use LinkedIn for Internships and Jobs - Learn strategies to leverage LinkedIn as a powerful tool for networking and job search.
  • Developing Leadership Qualities - Acquire leadership skills and qualities that employers value in potential candidates.
  • Mental Models for Decision Making- Understand decision-making frameworks to make informed career choices and strategic decisions.
  • Introspecting about One's Career - Reflect on your career goals, values, and aspirations to align your job search with your long-term vision.
  • Resume Review Sessions - Receive personalized feedback and guidance on your resume from our experienced mentors.
  • 4 Mock Interviews - Participate in mock interviews to practice and refine your interview skills with feedback from industry experts.
  • 3 Career Planning Sessions - Gain insights and guidance on career planning, goal setting, and job search strategies from our experienced mentors.
